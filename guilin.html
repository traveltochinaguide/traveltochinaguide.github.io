Guilin

Guilin travel guide — Li River cruises, karst peaks, Reed Flute Cave and travel tips.

Quick overview

Guilin's landscape is the stuff of classic Chinese paintings. Located in Southern China, it is world-renowned for its otherworldly karst topography.

Top attractions

  • Li River cruise — The classic way to experience Guilin's peaks and villages.
  • Reed Flute Cave — Stunning limestone cave with colorful lighting.
  • Yangshuo — Scenic countryside for biking, rock climbing and river activities.

Getting there

Guilin Liangjiang International Airport (KWL) has domestic flights; trains connect Guilin to many major cities.

Suggested itineraries

1 day: Li River short cruise + Reed Flute Cave.

2–3 days: Li River cruise to Yangshuo, cycling and countryside exploration.
