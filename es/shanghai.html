Shanghái

A dazzling metropolis showcasing China's futuristic ambition and colonial past.

Shanghai, a global financial hub, is a city of superlatives. Its futuristic skyline, dominated by the Shanghai Tower and the Oriental Pearl TV Tower, is a sight to behold.

Stroll along the Bund to admire the colonial-era architecture, explore the trendy boutiques and art galleries in the French Concession, or find tranquility in the classical Yu Garden. Shanghai is a testament to China's rapid development and a must-visit for any traveler.
