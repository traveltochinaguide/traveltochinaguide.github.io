Yangtsé

Cruise through the majestic Three Gorges, a lifeline of Chinese civilization.

The Yangtze is China's longest river, famous for the dramatic Three Gorges and popular river cruises that combine scenery with cultural visits.

Highlights

  • Three Gorges – steep-sided gorges carved by the river offering spectacular views.
  • Yangtze Cruises – multi-day cruise options between Chongqing and Yichang.
  • Cultural stops – riverside towns, temples and local markets.
