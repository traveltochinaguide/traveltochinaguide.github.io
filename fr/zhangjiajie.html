Zhangjiajie

Zhangjiajie travel guide –explore the pillar-like sandstone peaks, national parks and hiking routes.

Zhangjiajie is famous for its towering pillar-like sandstone formations set within a UNESCO-listed national park – an otherworldly landscape perfect for hiking and photography.

Highlights

  • Zhangjiajie National Forest Park – home to the famous quartz-sandstone pillars.
  • Avatar Hallelujah Mountains – scenic viewpoints inspired cinematic landscapes.
  • Glass Bridge (Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon) – a thrilling suspended walkway with dramatic views.
