Guangzhou

The heart of Cantonese culture, cuisine, and commerce in southern China.

Guangzhou, the capital of Guangdong Province, is the birthplace of Cantonese culture, cuisine, and the famous "Cantonese way of life." The city blends a millennium of trading history with a hyper-modern urban core.

Visit the Canton Tower for panoramic city views, explore Shamian Island's colonial European architecture, or feast on dim sum in the city's countless teahouses.
