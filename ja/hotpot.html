Hotpot

Social Dining at its Best

Hot pot or hotpot, also known as steamboat, is a cooking method that originates from China, prepared with a simmering pot of soup stock at the dining table, containing a variety of East Asian foodstuffs and ingredients.

Typical ingredients include thinly sliced meat, leaf vegetables, mushrooms, won tons, egg dumplings, tofu, and seafood. The cooked food is usually eaten with a dipping sauce.
