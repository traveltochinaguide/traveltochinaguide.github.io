Jiuzhaigou

Jiuzhaigou travel guide — colorful lakes, waterfalls and alpine valleys.

Quick overview

Jiuzhaigou is renowned for its vivid turquoise lakes, multi-tiered waterfalls and pristine alpine valleys — a scenic gem in Sichuan province.

Highlights

  • Rize Valley — colorful lakes and clear water reflections.
  • Shuzheng Valley — classic views of layered waterfalls.
  • Hiking & Photography — boardwalk trails provide easy access to viewpoints.
